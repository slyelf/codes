What is the Elder Scrolls?

The Elder Scrolls is a series of games developed by the company "Bethesda Game Studios" and published by "Bethesda Softworks." The game series consists of 8 seperate games and around 9 seperate DLC (Downloadable Content). The games are a series of role-playing, open world, fantasy video games. The 8 sperate games are:

The Elder Scrolls Games
Game Name Year
The Elder Scrolls I: Arena 1994
The Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall 1996
The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind 2002
The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion 2006
The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim 2011
The Elder Scrolls: Online 2014
