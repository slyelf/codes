The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Ideas

Ideas
 My goal is to play Skyrim in a roleplay manner. I plan to play the Main Questline, the Dragonborn DLC, the Dawnguard DLC as well as the thieves guild and the dark brotherhood later when my character goes a bit more insane later down the track. I would like my character to start of as a 'good' guy but then later become more insane as he/she goes through various experiences. Through the use of various mods I would like to be able to survive using the hardcore preset of frostfall, hunterborn and realistic needs and diseases. Using mods that add guilds I can also become a bard which I could use a let go to the all the insanity and become more normal after the accidents. Other guilds would be the hunting guild just to name a few. I would like to start off as an outcast and slowly work my way up to become a noble. I would also like to play as a werewolf and later turn into a werewolf but if I get turned into vampire before this then I will stay as a vampire until a cure is made.
